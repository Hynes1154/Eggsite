What is an Egg?

            An egg is the organic vessel containing the zygote
            in which an embryo develops until it can survive on its own,
            at which point the animal hatches.

Why we need Egg

            Eggs are useful sources of some of the hard to get nutrients like
             vitamins D and B12 as well as the mineral iodine.
            They are regarded as a 'complete' source of protein as they
            contain all nine essential amino acids.
